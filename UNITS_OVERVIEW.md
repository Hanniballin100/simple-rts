# Unit Roster Overview

A working reference for every trainable unit, grouped by faction, with role,
purpose, and playstyle — plus a redundancy audit flagging cut candidates.
Stats shown are the *base* numbers from `data.js` before the global `PACE`
multiplier (hp ×1.35, speed ×0.85, buildTime ×1.2).

Legend: **DPS-ish** = dmg ÷ cooldown. "req: tech" = needs the tech lab.

## Redundancy audit — cut / merge candidates

Ranked by how little each unit adds.

1. **Deep State duplicate air & extras** — Black Drone, Black Helicopter, Riot,
   HAARP, B-1, Mechanic, AC-130, B-2 are all shared verbatim with Globalists.
   *Option:* give Deep State 1–2 signature swaps (e.g. a stealth drone variant)
   or accept them as a "Globalist skin" and cut nothing but document it.
2. **Probe Drone (Grey)** — one-shot tracker, heavy micro, low payoff. **Cut.**
3. **Seismograph Dowser (Hollow)** — detector role largely covered by the
   Seismic Sense passive. **Cut** (or fold detection into the Vril Priestess).
4. **Cattle Mutilator (Grey)** — corpse-to-minerals on a faction that already
   runs on structure income. **Cut** or merge its scavenge into another unit.
5. **Mender Orb OR Vivisector (Grey)** — two repair units. Keep Vivisector
   (more unique: leech), **cut Mender Orb from Grey** (keep it for Reptilians).
6. **Street Preacher (Flat)** — marginal building-damage aura. **Cut or merge**
   into the Militia/Truck identity.
7. **Crop Duster** — shared Flat/Resistance toxin plane; overlaps Bio Bomber.
   Keep (it's each faction's only spray) but consider one shared art.
8. **Surveillance Van in Globalist extras** — it's Deep State's core vehicle;
   the Black SUV already fills Globalist's car slot. **Cut from Globalist.**
9. **Hollow bloat** — 16 units. Candidates beyond the Dowser: fold Guardian +
   Cave Saurian (both heavy melee) or Magma Mortar's role. Pick one to trim.

Nothing here is decided — this is the menu to choose from.
